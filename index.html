What This Means

Heating Degree Days (HDD) measure how cold the weather is. More HDD = colder weather = more heating needed.

2025 was 9% colder than 2023, yet we still achieved massive savings. This proves our improvements work in real Yukon winter conditions.

Dá Kų - Our House

"Our House" in Southern Tutchone (Dän k'è)

More than a building—Dá Kų is where our community gathers, where youth learn traditional practices, where elders share stories, and where the Dakwäkäda Dancers have performed for over 10 years.

Every dollar saved on energy is a dollar invested in our people. By reducing our building's environmental footprint, we honor our ancestors' tradition of careful stewardship while ensuring Dá Kų can serve future generations.

Environmental Stewardship

Following our ancestors who cared for this land for 8,000+ years, we reduce our footprint and lead by example.

Fiscal Responsibility

Responsible management means stretching every dollar to serve our citizens and programs.

Community Pride

CAFN leads the way for First Nations, showing what's possible with strategic investment.
